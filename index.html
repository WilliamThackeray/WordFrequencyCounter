Word Frequency Counter

This word frequency counter is simple to use but very versatile. It is very useful for analizing papers for specific word patterns or for checking your own writing for repeated vocabulary.

Using the site is easy. In the custom words input box, type the specific words you want to look for. These are the words the frequency counter will look for. For every word you type, click the 'enter' key and it will add them to the Custome Words box next to it. If you have a typo or want to delete a word from the box, just click it to delete it. You can clear all the words by clicking the 'Clear Words' button. When you are ready, click the 'Scan for Words' button and it will show you the results below.
